### steps to register webhook to Stripe
 -> Developers → Webhooks
 -> Click "Add endpoint"
 -> paste https://maroon-magpie-775843.hostingersite.com/stripe/webhook

Under "Select events", add these:
- checkout.session.completed
- invoice.payment_succeeded
- invoice.payment_failed
- customer.subscription.updated
- customer.subscription.deleted
- payment_intent.succeeded
- payment_intent.payment_failed
- charge.refunded          (added for FaithStack payout hold — flags a payout if refunded)
- charge.dispute.created   (added for FaithStack payout hold — flags a payout if disputed/chargeback)

On the next screen, click "Reveal" next to Signing secret — copy that value
